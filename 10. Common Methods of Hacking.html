10 Common Methods of Hacking

      2. A virus within something you download or install.

      4. Monitoring you without you knowing.

    4. Fake WAP :

      5. Fake WIFI to get access to your data.

      6. Location with more traffic.

      7. Flooding traffic to crash your site.

      8. Malware that tracks your keypad.

    8. Social Engineering :

      9. Psychological manipulation of people into performing actions or divulging confidential information.

        Example : Fake phone calls acting as Gov. or Businesses.

    9. Bait and Switch :

      10. Fake ads that take you to a page with a virus.

    10. Cookie Theft :

      11. Take cookies off your computer to act like its you.
