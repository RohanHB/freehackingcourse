What Exactly is Cybersecurity and Ethical Hacking

  • Cybersecurity put simply the act of taking certain measures to ensure that a network and its information is safe.

  • Examples of implementing Cybersecurity include,

    1. Using a Firewall

    2. Requiring strong passwords to login to computers

    3. Installing an Antivirus Software

  • Cybersecurity as a whole focuses on protecting a network from potential attacks and dangers.

  • Cybersecurity includes both offensive actions like Ethical Hacking and defensive actions like Blue Teaming.

  • Ethical Hacking is the act of attempting to break into a network in order to uncover vulnerabilities that may be present.

  • Even though it is technically hacking, it is legal hacking and all testing is done according to previously agreed upon rules.

  • Ethical Hacking falls under the umbrella of Cybersecurity and is an integral part of any Cybersecurity program.

  • Ethical Hacking is a part of Cybersecurity that tests a network's defenses against attacks that hackers would be launching.
